# Wise Workout — Project Description

**Project Topic:** CSIT-26-S2-05: A Mobile Application for Wise Workout
**Project Group:** FYP-26-S2-37

## Overview

Develop a mobile application (for Android and iOS) that can collect exercise data from cell-phone sensors or wearable devices, provide training and fitness advice, and facilitate social interactions such as initiating competitions between registered users.

The team will develop a SaaS product based on a suitable business model with a responsive, user-friendly interface that works seamlessly across mobile devices. The user experience must be optimised for different screen sizes.

## Core Features

### 1. Collect exercise data
Collect user exercise information from either the cell phone's own sensors or from smart wearable devices (via Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, etc.).

### 2. Estimate exercise effects and provide analysis
Estimate exercise results for each activity, day, week, month, or any period — including calories burned, average and maximum heart rate, etc. Cover both short-term and long-term effects.

### 3. Supply fitness advice and schedule a fitness plan
Provide fitness advice tailored to the user's fitness goals and entered health information. Generate a fitness plan based on the goal, and let users customise it (e.g. plan length, daily weight-loss target).

### 4. Remind user to exercise or take a break
Send reminders to exercise when scheduled goals are not met, and reminders to rest when the user has been over-exercising.

### 5. Connect with social media and initiate competitions
Share exercise data on Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, TikTok. Users can initiate competitions with other registered users and invite them to join. Competition results and rankings can be shared on social media.

## Product Requirements

The team will develop a SaaS product with the above functionalities, offering a user-friendly solution that provides **profile-based services**. The product must empower users to make **data-driven decisions** based on their profile and the activities they carry out.

The application must include:

- **User profiling**
- **Intelligent content delivery** with context-aware recommendations
- **Incentive-based motivation mechanism** with gamification to facilitate social interaction
- **Analytics** — progress tracking, predictive analytics, etc.
- **Product marketing website** to attract users to sign up and download the mobile application

The team may consider releasing the application to the app store.
